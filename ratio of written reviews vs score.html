Ratio of Text Reviews to All Reviews vs Average Rating

Visualization of the total number of ratings vs. total text reviews of each book in the Good Reads library.

For a final exploration of the data on Good Reads, we wanted to take what we learned from the last graph examining a ratio of written reviews to just scored reviews vs the Average rating.

The purpose of this was to explore how people review books. Seeing If doing a written review in addition to giving a star rating would change the outcome of a score. For example perhaps some books would simply receive a bombardment of one star reviews from people not interested in leaving a text review but instead just want the score to go down.

From the data, we were not able to disprove the null hypothesis which is: A books rating is not corelated to the number of text reviews compared to the number of non-text reviews.
